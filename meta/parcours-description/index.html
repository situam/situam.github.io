parcours-description

Why?
—
A fascination for meandering attention in soft fascinations and the ever-expanding accessibility of DIY sound processors invites an experimental process of real-time soundscape mediation.

How?
—
In a cyclic process of meander-experimenting through slowly changing environments and capturing moments in-between, serendipity is embraced. Listening and responding, recording, listening again, mediating experience in situ.

What?
—
situ.am is a live process archive, a open-source collection of tools for DIY in situ soundscape mediation.
